Team Rocket

Mission

Our mission is to improve the efficiency of in-space small rocket engines by leveraging the additive manufacturing process to reduce mass and increase the lifespan of components.

Introducing Space Buddy

Named for its small size and large goals, Space Buddy is our novel 3D-printed regeneratively cooled rocket designed by four mechanical engineering undergraduates for their senior project.
